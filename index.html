PRADHAN MANTRI AWAS YOJNA

HOUSING FOR ALL

Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana Mission launched on 25th June 2015 which intends to provide housing for all in urban areas by year 2022.

Ministry of Housing & Urban Affairs has initiated Affordable Rental Housing Complexes (ARHCs), a sub-scheme under Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana.

This will provide ease of living to urban migrants/poor in Industrial Sector as well as in non-formal urban economy to get access to dignified affordable rental housing close to their workplace.
